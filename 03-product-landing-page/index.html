The BeanStalk

Book Store

The BeanStalk will offer a wide range of book, magazine, and music selections. This includes just about every conceivable category including fiction, non-fiction, business, science, children's, hobbies, collecting, and other types of books. Our music selection will concentrate on vinyl. We will also be offering a competitive buy and trade service. In addition, we offer a search and order service for customer seeking to find hard to get items. The BeanStalk is a comfortable "reading room" type atmosphere that encourages relaxation and focus with ample furniture and study space.

Coffee Shop

The BeanStalk's coffee shop offers its customers the best prepared coffee, espresso, cappuchinos, lattes, teas, and pastries to keep you sated while you read!

About

We believe that bookstores are essential to a healthy culture. They’re where authors can connect with readers, where we discover new writers, where children get hooked on the thrill of reading that can last a lifetime. They’re also anchors for our downtowns and communities.
